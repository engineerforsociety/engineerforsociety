# **App Name**: Engineer For Society

## Core Features:

- Community Forums: Discussion boards for new engineers, alternative pathways, math & science support, industry discussions, and project showcases.
- Social Impact Coordination: Features for blood donation drives, community events, mentorship matching, and skills exchange.
- Engineering Podcast Hub: Centralized platform for audio content on engineering methods, careers, and innovation.
- Resource Library: Collection of technical documents, career guides, and educational materials. Users are able to generate summaries of long form documentation using the 'Summarize' tool, shortening research time.
- Local Chapters & Events: Geographic communities for in-person events, workshops, and professional development sessions.
- User Profiles: Detailed profiles for engineers to showcase their background, interests, and contributions.
- Job Board: Job postings from corporate partners. The LLM based job matcher will match you with potential job prospects based on your profile.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Blue (#1F3A5F) for the header, navigation, and primary buttons, symbolizing trust and engineering credibility.
- Secondary color: Teal (#2FA4A9) for highlights, links, and icons, representing social responsibility and collaboration.
- Accent color: Warm Orange (#F4A261) for important buttons and event tags, adding warmth and encouraging action.
- Neutral colors: Off-white (#F8F9FA) for background, white (#FFFFFF) for cards/panels, dark gray (#2B2B2B) for primary text, medium gray (#6C757D) for secondary text, and light gray (#E0E0E0) for borders.
- Headline Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for headings, navigation, and buttons; chosen for its modern and technical design suitable for screen readability.
- Body Font: 'Source Sans 3' (sans-serif) for paragraphs, forum posts, and long-form content, providing excellent readability with a friendly, human touch.
- Code Font: 'JetBrains Mono' (monospace) for code, equations, or technical snippets.
- Use clean, professional icons that represent each feature category. Icons should align with the overall design language, and balance technical precision with a welcoming feel.
